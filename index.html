[1] About me

My name is Cosmin Conceatu and I am a Front-end Developer based in Bucharest, Romania.

Although I've finished a finance school, I was lucky to discover that my real passion doesn't sit around economy, but more around tech and design. I love building good looking websites and creating memorable user experiences.

I began my web development journey in May 2019 and since then I decided to invest my time and energy in front-end development and UX design.

When I'm not coding, I am a bedroom DJ and when I'm not reading documentations, I am reading books.
